Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to your most common questions about the Robotics Club

What is the Robotics Club, and what do you participate in?

The Robotics Club is dedicated to fostering creativity and innovation in robotics. We host workshops, projects, and competitions to help members build and learn.

Do I need prior experience or knowledge to join? Can anyone join it?

No, we welcome members from all backgrounds, whether you are a beginner or an expert in robotics! Even teachers were once students!

How can I participate in club activities?

Simply sign up on our website or visit us during club hours. Stay updated on events through our social media channels.

Are there any membership fees?

No, joining the club is free. However, some workshops or projects might have material costs.

Can I suggest ideas for projects?

Absolutely! We encourage members to bring forward their ideas and collaborate with the team to bring them to life.

What types of robotics projects and competitions does the club engage in?

The club has its own special teams or FFCS teams who are each working on a project. The types of projects are limitless as long as they are well-founded. The club participates in competitions, even at an international level, which means that we do not limit ourselves to any level when it comes to competitions.

Do I need to own a robotics kit or equipment to participate in the club activities?

In most cases, you wouldn't need to buy your own equipment if you are participating in events, but if it's a project, then it might be worked out of pocket, which would require some equipment in advance.

Are there any mentorship programs or opportunities for members to learn from experienced individuals in the field?

Our faculty coordinator, Dr. Arockiasamy S, has many connections in this field. We also have a deeply rooted network of alumni of this club who are always willing to help. There are also regular boot camps hosted to help develop the level of members in this club.

How can I get involved in leadership roles within the Robotics Club?

If you are active enough in the club and can demonstrate your value and vision for the club's future to the leaders or the board, you could be selected as a leader the next time they are recruiting for such roles.

Can the Robotics Club help me pursue a career in robotics or engineering?

Certainly! The Robotics Club offers opportunities to gain practical skills, knowledge, and networking connections that can greatly benefit your pursuit of a career in robotics or engineering.
